About Us

Aims/Goals :


1. To provide congenial atmosphere / environment for teaching learning process.

2. To train children to be self-sufficient in their future life.

3. To assist and provide opportunities for their mental, intellectual, spiritual growth in all walks of life.

4. To emphasis on value education to inculcate the desirable values like honesty, dedication, competitive spirit/belongings; discipline, leadership, co-existence, social adjustment, hard work etc., which is need of the day.

5. To achieve excellence in both scholastic and non-scholastic activities, habits and cultural heritage.

6. To help for choosing career in future through Career Guidance Programmes extensive lecture series for parents & students.

7. School to be a community center of guidance.

8. To prepare students for competition and prove their worth in competitive era.

9. To create responsible, trustworthy, duty-bound, reliable and good citizens of tomorrow, of our motherland INDIA.

Curriculum :

Gurukul Public School is based on CBSC curricula. The School, though an English Medium School, gives equal importance to teaching of Indian languages such as Maithili, Hindi and Sanskrit.

The wholesome curriculum of the school provides plenty of opportunities for the intellectual, social, physical, emotional, moral and spiritual development of child. However, all these can be achieved successfully by bearing collective responsibilities by parents, teachers and students.
